Main features

Meet Groningen will be your guide as you go on quests to discover the city of Groningen. We will show you the highlights that you don't want to miss!

  • Discover amazing places.
  • Learn about the city as you go.
  • Quizzes to test your knowledge.
  • Earn points with your progress.
  • Create your own quests with your own locations.
  • Have fun!

Why choose our app

Are you new to the city of Groningen? Or do you simply want to learn more about it? Then we have just what you need! No need for expensive tour guides or getting lost trying to find your way around! Our app allows you to plan ahead your tour of the city and start exploring whenever you want. Furthermore, if a rain storm decides to interrupt your tour (we are in the Netherlands after all!!), no worries! You can always take cover and continue when the sun shines again!
What are you waiting for? Join us in the adventure of a lifetime!
